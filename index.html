People

The Electrical Engineering research area of DEIB counts 25 faculty staff members (full professors, associate professors, assistant professors) and about 20 temporary researchers, including PhD students, assistant professors and research collaborators.


Research

The research area of the Electrical Engineering has two main goals: – investigation and solutions of theoretical and experimental problems related to electrical phenomena; – promotion of the knowledge and advancements in the Electrical Engineering. The area is deeply interdisciplinary and is mainly focused on theoretical aspects and applications to the fields of electromagnetism and more broadly in all the aspects connected to the Electrical Engineering. The area promotes the advancement and the exploitation of sustainable mobility, industrial electronics for energy conversion, signal processing, measurement, diagnostic methods, EMC and EMI analysis to ensure an efficient and innovative management of power systems.


Laboratories

The Electrical Engineering area is equipped with 7 laboratiories with state-of-the-art equipment to carry out research and teaching activities.


TEACHING

The development and advancement of the Electrical Engineering degree programs are a vital component for both the social and industrial progress of the field. The research area offers excellent educational opportunities, strongly supports the Bachelor and the Master programs in Electrical Engineering. Additionally, there are special programs reserved to Electrical Engineering students. Among these, are worth of note the Double M.Sc. degree programs with the chinese universities Xi’An, Xi’an Jiaotong University (XJTU), Pechino, Beihang University (BUAA) and Shanghai Jiaotong University (SJTU) .


PHD

The Electrical Engineering area also offers a PhD course which has an international reach and influence.
